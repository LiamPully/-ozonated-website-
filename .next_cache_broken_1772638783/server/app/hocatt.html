What to Expect

We begin with intake screening, hydration guidance, and individualized setup to align the chamber program with your goals.

Pre-Session Checklist

  • • Hydrate well before arrival.
  • • Avoid heavy meals 1 to 2 hours before your session.
  • • Wear comfortable clothing and remove metallic accessories.
  • • Inform the team about medications, implants, pregnancy, or active medical concerns.
  • • Arrive 10 minutes early for protocol preparation.

Post-Session Instructions

  • • Continue hydration for the rest of the day.
  • • Keep activities light if you feel fatigued.
  • • Avoid alcohol immediately after your treatment.
  • • Track your recovery response over 24 hours.
  • • Follow your recommended session cadence for best outcomes.

Sports Enhancement Benefits

  • • Supports post-training recovery windows
  • • May assist circulation and oxygen efficiency
  • • Helps manage muscle fatigue during intense cycles
  • • Complements conditioning and performance programs
